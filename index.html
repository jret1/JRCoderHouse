HERE WE PRESENT JOAN RETAMERO

"Never shying away from straining the boundaries of his sound, blossoming Argentinian producer and DJ Joan Retamero continues to shine in recent times. His knack to create distinct and immersive atmospheres via various refined strains of house, and blurring this with techno textures, he has earned himself an array of releases on acclaimed labels, such as Paul Oakenfold’s Perfecto Records, also featuring on his "Sunset at Stonehenge" compilation, he also released on Hernan Cattaneo’s SUDBEAT. His innovative sound has landed him gigs in hotspot cities and clubs around the world including Ministry Of Sound, Miami, Barcelona, Amsterdam, E1, Pacha and many more, all alongside many gigs in his native country, residing in the most sought after spots in the country. His versatile sound has no limits as he enjoys exploring the cinematic and experimental ends of the spectrum."
